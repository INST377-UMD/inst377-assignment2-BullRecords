Upon loading of the window, Voice Recognition will begin. After giving your browser access to your microphone, you gain access to a couple features. 1st, simply say "navigate to (desired page)" and you will access it. 2nd, say "hello" and enjoy the response. 3rd, say "Change the color to (color)" and the background will change to that color. This feature can be turned off and on via the buttons to the right. This page also has a feature where you can say "Load Dog Breed [Breed]" and it will give you information on that breed. Give it a try!!
